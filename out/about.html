Our Mission

To empower Tamil-speaking students in Sri Lanka with cutting-edge IT skills through industry-focused education, practical training, and career support. We are committed to bridging the gap between traditional learning and modern technology demands.

Our Vision

To become the leading IT education platform in Sri Lanka, recognized for excellence in Tamil-medium technology training. We envision a future where every Tamil-speaking student has access to world-class IT education and career opportunities.

Our Story

A journey of passion, innovation, and commitment to transforming IT education in Sri Lanka

2019
2019

The Beginning

MasterBrain was founded with a vision to make quality IT education accessible to Tamil-speaking students in Sri Lanka. We started with a small team of passionate educators.

2020
2020

First Milestone

Launched our first comprehensive course in React.js, attracting over 100 students in the first batch. The response was overwhelming and validated our mission.

2021-2022
2021-2022

Rapid Growth

Expanded our course offerings to include Next.js, Node.js, and .NET. Our student community grew to over 500 active learners, and we introduced live interactive sessions.

2023-Present
2023-Present

Leading the Way

Today, MasterBrain is recognized as a leading IT education platform with over 10,000 students, 50+ courses, and a 98% success rate. We continue to innovate and expand.
